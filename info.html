JEE (Main) Exam

Details
Purpose Admission to undergraduate engineering programs at NITs, IIITs, other centrally funded technical institutions, and state-level colleges. It is also a qualifying exam for JEE Advanced.
Exam Mode Computer-based Test (CBT), except for the Drawing section in Paper 2A (B.Arch) which is offline.
Exam Duration 3 hours for Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech) and Paper 2B (B.Planning).
Marking Scheme
  • MCQs: +4 for each correct answer, -1 for each incorrect answer.
  • Numerical Value Questions (NVQs): +4 for each correct answer, -1 for each incorrect answer.
Subjects & Questions
  • **Paper 1 (B.E./B.Tech):** 75 questions total (25 each from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics). Each subject has 20 MCQs and 5 NVQs, with all questions being compulsory.
  • **Paper 2A (B.Arch):** 77 questions total from Mathematics, Aptitude Test, and Drawing.
  • **Paper 2B (B.Planning):** 100 questions total from Mathematics, Aptitude Test, and Planning-based questions.

NEET (UG) Exam

Details
Purpose Admission to undergraduate medical (MBBS), dental (BDS), and AYUSH (BAMS, BUMS, BHMS, etc.) courses in India.
Exam Mode Offline (Pen-and-paper mode).
Exam Duration 3 hours (180 minutes).
Marking Scheme
  • +4 for each correct answer.
  • -1 for each incorrect answer.
  • 0 for unattempted questions.
Subjects & Questions 180 compulsory Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs) total. 45 questions each from Physics and Chemistry, and 90 questions from Biology (Botany & Zoology).

KCET Exam

Details
Purpose Admission to undergraduate courses in Engineering, Technology, B. Pharma, B. Arch, and other professional programs in colleges across Karnataka.
Exam Mode Offline (Pen-and-paper mode).
Exam Duration 80 minutes for each subject paper.
Marking Scheme
  • +1 for each correct answer.
  • There is **no negative marking** for incorrect answers.
  • No marks will be awarded for multiple answers to a single question.
Subjects & Questions 180 questions total (60 questions each from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics). Each subject paper is worth 60 marks.
